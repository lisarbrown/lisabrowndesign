Hourly Hire Car
Selection

Web | UX/UI

Problem

Through analysing user interactions with an existing landing page, it was discovered that there was a high volume of “rage clicks” on a section of the page that was not interactive. This indicates users were expecting further product information that was not available

Solution

Using the same design which users already thought was interactive, I added in modal elements to appear when the car was clicked - these provided additional product information in a digestible format that users could easily click out of and move between cars in the grid to compare.
