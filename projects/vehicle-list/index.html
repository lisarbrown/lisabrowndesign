Car auctioning company:

Vehicles List

Web | UX/UI

Background

Arnold Clark bought Central Car Auctions in 2022, since then we’ve been working with agency’s who have built the Central Car Auctions website to update and rebrand with the new style.

Problem

I was asked by the stakeholder to refresh the current 'Vehicles List' page and to pull out some of the key vehicle features through to this page aswell. Currently the features show in the ‘Vehicle Product’ page, however, this change would allow customer to get a preview of these details without having to click through to the product page.

Solution

After researching how other retail websites show their stock online, I was able to create a clear layout using a card design incorporating all of the main points the stakeholder had requested, such as making the sale date a feature, the vendor of the car and making the image of the car larger. I done this by highlighting different key information with colour and icons and switching to a portrait card layout to make it easier for the user to compare the cars.

Lessons/to improve

try fit it in a smaller card suggest to the stakeholder less content Work closer with the developer to avoid conflicting styles
